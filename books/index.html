The Lie We Were Taught
// Book 1 of the Learning To Be Human Series

The Core Question: "What if we've misunderstood the purpose of life?"

Most of what we know about success, happiness, and our place in the world is inherited — built on a foundation of "lies" we were taught before we had the tools to question them. The Lie We Were Taught is an invitation to unlearn, to strip away the noise, and to re-examine what it means to be truly human.
